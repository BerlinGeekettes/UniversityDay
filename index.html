University Edition

November 1, 2013, Berlin

Collaborative learning

For Students and Graduates

What to Expect?

This is a one day mini summit gathering Berlin’s technologists and 200 women from surrounding technology and design universities. Attendees who participate will hear from over 30 speakers from both large tech companies and well as established tech startups. We hope you will join us for this exciting day of learning and connecting.

Speakers Lineup

Learn from top professionals behind Berlin and Germany’s most innovative tech companies. This panel and workshop packed event will present topics that range from job search strategy, how to land a job at a large tech company to how to found your own startup after graduation.
